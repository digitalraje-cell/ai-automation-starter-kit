# Real-World Lead Automation Example

## Scenario

A coach runs a webinar funnel and wants to automate lead communication after registration.

---

## Workflow Overview

Landing Page → Form Submission → CRM Entry → WhatsApp Message → Email Sequence → Sales Notification

---

## Step-by-Step Automation Logic

### Step 1 — User Registers

Trigger:
User fills registration form.

Action:
Store lead in CRM.

---

### Step 2 — Send WhatsApp Welcome

Message:
Hi {{name}}, thank you for registering for the webinar.

We will send reminders before the session.

---

### Step 3 — Send Email Confirmation

Email Subject:
Webinar Registration Confirmed

Email Content:
Welcome email with webinar details.

---

### Step 4 — Reminder Automation

1 hour before webinar:
Send WhatsApp reminder.

15 minutes before webinar:
Send final reminder email.

---

### Step 5 — Notify Sales Team

After webinar ends:

Send notification to sales team with:

- Lead name
- Email
- Phone number

---

## Tools That Can Be Used

- GoHighLevel
- Zapier
- Make (Integromat)
- WhatsApp API
- Email Automation Tools

---

## Benefits of This Workflow

- Improves webinar attendance
- Reduces manual work
- Increases lead conversion
- Ensures consistent communication
