# AI Chatbot Qualification Flow

## Objective

Automatically qualify leads before booking a sales call.

---

## Workflow

User Message → Chatbot Greeting → Qualification Questions → Score Lead → Book Appointment → Notify Sales Team

---

## Questions Asked

1. What is your business type?
2. What is your monthly revenue?
3. What automation tools do you currently use?
4. What is your biggest challenge?

---

## Lead Qualification Logic

High Score:
Send booking link.

Medium Score:
Send follow-up resources.

Low Score:
Send nurturing content.

---

## Tools Supported

- WhatsApp Chatbot
- Web Chatbot
- CRM Integration
- Appointment Booking Tools
